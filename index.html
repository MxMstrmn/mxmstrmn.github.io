I’m currently pursuing a PhD at TUM, conducting research in the labs of Prof. Fabian Theis and Prof. Stephan Günnemann. My work primarily involves generative models, mostly on graphs, applied to real data in the domain of single-cell genomics and a special focus on drug discovery. So far, I’ve explored perturbation modelling and molecule generation, and recently I also became intrigued by the prospects of protein design.
